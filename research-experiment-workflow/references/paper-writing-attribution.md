# Paper Writing Attribution

The `paper-writing-*` references in this skill are adapted from the MIT-licensed repository `Master-cai/Research-Paper-Writing-Skills`:

- Repository: <https://github.com/Master-cai/Research-Paper-Writing-Skills>
- Upstream skill: `research-paper-writing/`
- Upstream license: MIT License, copyright (c) 2026 Master-cai

The upstream README states that most writing knowledge and methodology comes from Prof. Peng Sida's open study notes, while the repository contribution is organization, structured adaptation, and packaging as reusable skills.

This repository adapts the writing guidance into the artifact-gated `research-experiment-workflow` skill. The writing guidance does not replace the local evidence gates: paper claims must still be traceable to saved artifacts, analyses, figures, tables, or citations.
